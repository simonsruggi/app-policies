# ZenFlip

**Focus better. Flip your phone. Get things done.**

ZenFlip turns your phone face-down to start a Pomodoro focus session. Stay productive with gamification, streaks, and detailed stats.

## Key Features

- **Flip to Focus** — Place your phone face-down to start a timed focus session automatically
- **Pomodoro Timer** — Built-in Pomodoro technique with customizable work and break intervals
- **Gamification & Streaks** — Earn rewards, maintain daily streaks, and level up your focus habits
- **Detailed Statistics** — Track your productivity over time with charts and session history
- **Distraction-Free** — No ads, no tracking, just pure focus
