## Description

FarmaStock is a personal inventory management tool for medicines and para-medicines. It allows you to scan barcodes, track expiry dates, and receive notifications.

FarmaStock offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions:

- **Premium Yearly**: Billed yearly

Subscriptions unlock unlimited product tracking (free tier: 15 products).

Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID Account Settings.
