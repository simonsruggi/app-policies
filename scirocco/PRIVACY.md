# Scirocco - Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 15, 2026**

## Overview

Scirocco ("the App") is developed by Simone Ruggiero. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**Scirocco does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What the App accesses:
- **Location**: The App may access your location for nautical navigation purposes. Location data is processed on-device and is not transmitted to any server.

### What the App does NOT do:
- We do not collect analytics or usage data
- We do not use advertising identifiers
- We do not share any data with third parties

## Third-Party Services

The App uses **RevenueCat** for managing in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data as described in their [Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

## Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
