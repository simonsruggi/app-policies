# Privacy Policy — PokerOdds

**Last updated: March 21, 2026**

## Information We Collect

PokerOdds does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data.

### Camera Usage
- The app uses your device's camera exclusively to recognize playing cards.
- Camera data is processed entirely on-device using Apple's Vision framework.
- No images are stored, recorded, or transmitted.

### Usage Data
- We do not use any analytics or tracking tools.
- We do not collect crash reports or usage statistics.

### Purchases
- In-app purchases (if any) are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store.
- We do not have access to your payment information.

## Data Storage
- All app data is stored **locally on your device**.
- No data is synced to any cloud service.
- Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

## Third-Party Services
- PokerOdds does not integrate with any third-party services.
- No data is shared with third parties.

## Children's Privacy
- PokerOdds does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
