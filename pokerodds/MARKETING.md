# PokerOdds: Scan Your Cards

**Know your odds before you bet.**

## Features

- **Instant Card Scanning** — Point your camera at your hole cards and get results in seconds
- **Real-Time Odds** — Pre-flop win percentages for all 169 Texas Hold'em starting hands
- **Multi-Way Pots** — Adjust opponent count from 1 to 9 and see how your odds change
- **On-Device Processing** — All card recognition happens locally, no internet needed
- **Clean Design** — Beautiful dark poker-themed interface
