# PhotoTrim

**Free up storage. Keep your memories.**

PhotoTrim compresses your photos to save space on your device without losing quality. Clean up your photo library in just a few taps.

## Key Features

- **Smart Compression** — Reduce photo file size while preserving visual quality
- **Batch Processing** — Compress multiple photos at once to save time
- **Storage Insights** — See exactly how much space your photos are taking up
- **Before & After** — Preview compressed photos before applying changes
- **Privacy First** — All processing happens on your device, nothing is uploaded
