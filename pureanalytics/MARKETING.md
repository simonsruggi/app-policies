# PureAnalytics

**Privacy-first website analytics, right from your iPhone.**

PureAnalytics gives you clean, simple website analytics without compromising your visitors' privacy. No cookies, no tracking scripts, just the insights you need.

## Key Features

- **Privacy-First** — No cookies, no invasive tracking, fully GDPR-compliant
- **Real-Time Dashboard** — Monitor page views, visitors, and referrers as they happen
- **Simple Setup** — Add a lightweight script to your site and start tracking in minutes
- **Beautiful iOS App** — Check your analytics on the go with a clean, native experience
- **Your Data, Your Control** — No data is sold or shared with third parties
