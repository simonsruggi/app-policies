# Privacy Policy — PureAnalytics

**Last updated: March 17, 2026**

## Information We Collect

PureAnalytics collects minimal data necessary to provide its analytics service.

### Account Data
- When you sign in with Google Sign-In, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. This information is used solely for authentication and account identification.

### Analytics Data
- PureAnalytics connects to a backend service (Supabase) to store and retrieve your website analytics data.
- The analytics data you collect through PureAnalytics (page views, visitor counts, referrers, etc.) is stored securely on our servers.

### What We Do NOT Collect
- We do not use any third-party tracking or advertising tools.
- We do not serve ads or share your data with advertisers.
- We do not collect crash reports or device usage statistics.

## Data Storage
- Account and analytics data is stored securely on Supabase servers.
- Your data is associated with your account and is not shared with other users.
- Deleting your account removes all associated data from our servers.

## Third-Party Services
- **Google Sign-In** — Used for authentication only. Google's privacy policy applies to the sign-in process: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Supabase** — Used as our backend database. Supabase's privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
- No data is sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Purchases
- In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store.
- We do not have access to your payment information.

## Children's Privacy
- PureAnalytics does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy
- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
